GAME INFORMATION

Co-Authors: Pragya Meshram, Aparna Sharma

Goal : you have to protect your spaceship from all the aliens ships by destroying them and nothing to say more .

Comment : This game is modified version of my previous game BAAZ.. INVADER which was inspired from game CHICKEN INVADER .

Keys : Hot Keys Esc: Quit Game Any Time

           F1 : Show help Any Time F4 : Toggle full screen.F5 : Save game.F6 : Load game.

           F8 : Change Background Music (More then 10).F10: Take snapshots of screen and save as bitmap.

           $$$ Also there are 3 keys for enabling cheat mode when game is running . Try to find out ???

 

Game Play : Mouse : To move around. Leftbutton: Fire (Don't Click , just press the left button to fire non stop)

Trouble Shooting:

1.No Sound : In the game 2 type of music files are used WAV and MIDI files . If your system does not have midi synthesizer or driver then the background music will not play. So nothing wrong in game.

2.Game Speed is too Slow : If the game speed is too slow in full screen then this may be due to your less vedio RAM. Press F4 to get out from fullscreen mode. This may solve your Problem.

System Requirements

Before attempting to play the game, please make sure your system matches or improves on the minimum specification. To fully appreciate all the features included in SPACE INVADER, it is suggested to use the RECOMMENDED PC specification or as close to it as possible.

Optimum:        Processor: PIII-750        OS: Win 95/98/ME/XP/NT        CD-ROM: 24X CD

       RAM: 128Mb       Controls: Keyboard , Mouse       Sound: PCI DirectX 7.0 compatible sound card with Surround Stereo Sound Speakers.        Graphics: 64Mb AGP Video card
